Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, you need to have completed 10+2 with Science stream (specific subjects depend on the course). Minimum percentage requirements and entrance test details are provided during the application process.

When does the admission process start? ‚ñº

We have two admission cycles per year - one starting in July and another in January. Applications are accepted 3 months prior to the start date. Early applications are encouraged as seats are limited.

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for male and female students with modern amenities, 24/7 security, mess facilities, and recreational areas. Hostel admission is subject to availability.
